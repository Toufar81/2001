Game Rules

  1. The player selects the winning score
  2. Each player starts with 0 points.
  3. In their turn, a player rolls 2 dice (standard D6 dice).
  4. The number of obtained pips is added to the total score.
  5. Starting from the second turn:
    • if a player rolls a 7, they divide their number of points by 7, disregarding the fractional part
    • if the player rolls an 11, they multiply the current number of points by 11.
  6. The first player to reach 501, 1001, 2001 points wins..
